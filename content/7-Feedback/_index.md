In this section, I would like to share my personal feedback and experiences during my participation in the **First Cloud AI Journey** program. The following opinions are provided to help improve the program and provide better experiences for future interns.


# Overall Evaluation


## 1. Working Environment

The working environment during the program was very friendly, open, and professional. The members of the program were always willing to support me whenever I faced difficulties during the learning process and project implementation.

The working atmosphere created a comfortable environment that allowed me to focus on researching new technologies and completing assigned tasks effectively. In addition, I hope that the program can organize more networking and community activities in the future to strengthen the connection among members.


## 2. Support from the Admin Team

The Admin team provided enthusiastic support throughout my internship. They always gave detailed guidance on how to approach problems, provided learning materials, and answered questions related to AWS, system architecture, and project development.

What I appreciated the most was that instead of directly providing solutions, the Admin team encouraged interns to analyze problems, investigate causes, and develop appropriate solutions by themselves. This approach helped me improve my problem-solving mindset and technical thinking.


## 3. Relevance of the Program to My Academic Major

The program was highly relevant to my Information Technology major. The curriculum not only strengthened my fundamental knowledge of computer networks, databases, and software development but also gave me opportunities to gain hands-on experience with AWS technologies.

Through developing the **IRMS (Incident Response Management System)** project, I was able to gain practical knowledge of **Cloud Architecture, Serverless Architecture, Security Monitoring, and AI Integration**, which I had limited opportunities to explore deeply at university.


## 4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities

During the internship, I gained many valuable skills, including:

- Using project management tools.
- Working effectively in a technology team environment.
- Writing technical documentation.
- Designing Cloud system architectures.
- Improving communication and work reporting skills.

Furthermore, the sharing sessions from industry experts and guest speakers provided me with practical insights into the roles of Cloud Engineers, DevOps Engineers, and AI Engineers, helping me better understand career development paths in the technology industry.


## 5. Learning Culture and Team Spirit

The learning culture within the program was very positive. Members always respected, supported, and shared knowledge with each other.

When facing challenging problems related to system design, AWS deployment, or project completion, everyone actively discussed and collaborated to find solutions.

This experience helped me not only improve my technical knowledge but also develop effective teamwork skills in a real working environment.


## 6. Internship Policies and Benefits

Having access to AWS resources for practical learning and being provided with high-quality learning materials were significant advantages for interns.

In addition, flexible working arrangements, guidance from experienced members, and opportunities to explore modern Cloud and AI technologies were extremely valuable experiences throughout the internship.


# Additional Feedback


## What did you find most satisfying during your internship?

The most satisfying experience was being able to directly participate in building a real-world AWS-based system through the **IRMS (Incident Response Management System)** project.

Instead of only studying theoretical concepts, I had the opportunity to design Cloud architecture, deploy AWS services, integrate an AI Assistant, and understand how to build a secure and scalable Serverless system.

Furthermore, the enthusiastic support from the Admin team and other members also contributed greatly to my positive internship experience.


## What do you think the program should improve for future interns?

I think the program could provide more detailed guidelines on system architecture design standards, especially regarding how to create professional Architecture Diagrams following real-world practices.

This would help new interns quickly understand professional documentation standards and produce higher-quality technical diagrams from the beginning of the program.


## If recommending this program to a friend, would you recommend it? Why?

I would definitely recommend this program to my friends.

For me, this was not only a valuable internship environment but also a great opportunity to experience real-world technologies, learn from experienced professionals, and develop a more professional working mindset.

The program motivated me to continue improving my AWS knowledge, developing Cloud skills, and creating a clearer career direction for my future.
