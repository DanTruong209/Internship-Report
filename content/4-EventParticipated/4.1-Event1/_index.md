# Event Experience

Participating in the **"GenAI-powered App-DB Modernization Workshop"** was an interesting and valuable experience that gave me the opportunity to connect with the technology community, observe professional presentations, and gain practical knowledge about **Cloud, AI, and Software Architecture**.


## Activities During the Event

During the event, I had the opportunity to participate in networking activities and interact with other attendees.

One of the interesting side activities was experiencing **Tử vi Đại Việt**. This activity created a friendly atmosphere, allowing participants to relax, communicate, and build connections with each other.


Besides the networking activities, I also attended presentations from speakers and participants about important technology topics, including:

- Application and database modernization.
- Domain-Driven Design (DDD).
- Event-Driven Architecture.
- Microservice Architecture.
- AI applications in software development.


## Observations and Learning from the Presentations

Through the presentations, I gained a better understanding of how engineers analyze problems and design real-world systems.

Some key lessons I learned include:

- Software development is not only about writing code; engineers need to understand business requirements before implementing solutions.
- System architecture should be designed with scalability, maintainability, and future expansion in mind.
- Technologies such as **Microservices** and **Event-Driven Architecture** help reduce dependencies between system components.
- AI can become a powerful assistant in the software development process, improving productivity and efficiency.


## Personal Lessons Learned

After attending the event, I realized that building a real-world system requires a combination of multiple factors:

- Strong technical fundamentals.
- System design thinking.
- Communication and knowledge-sharing skills.
- Active participation in technology communities to expand knowledge.


The event motivated me to continue learning AWS technologies and improving my internship project **IRMS (Incident Response Management System)**.

Especially, I can apply the knowledge gained from this event, including:

- Cloud Architecture.
- Serverless Architecture.
- Event-driven processing.
- AI Integration.

to the design and development of practical features in my project.
